# RepSearch Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 11, 2026 — DRAFT pending owner approval (D5/D6)._

RepSearch ("the app") is a workout logging and training-research app. This
policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights over it.

## Data we collect

- **Account data:** email address and password (handled by Supabase Auth; we
  never see your password), username, optional profile details (bio, training
  experience, goals).
- **Fitness data you log:** workouts (exercises, sets, reps, weights, RIR,
  tempo, range of motion, pain flags), bodyweight, body measurements, sleep,
  calorie and protein estimates, daily check-ins.
- **Content you post:** posts, comments, votes, shared workouts, templates and
  programs, and who you follow.

We do **not** collect your location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or any
data for tracking. The app contains no ads and no third-party analytics SDKs.

## How we use it

- To run the app: your history, charts, records, programs, and feed.
- **Research (opt-in only):** if you enable research participation, your
  training data is included in aggregated, anonymized statistics that power
  the Study feature for all users. It is never shown or shared in a form that
  identifies you, and you can opt out at any time in settings.
- We do not sell data or share it with advertisers.

## Visibility

Your fitness data is private by default. Per-field privacy toggles control
what appears on your public profile. Posts, comments, and anything you share
to the community feed are visible to other users.

## Storage & security

Data is stored with Supabase (hosted Postgres) and processed by the RepSearch
API server. Transport is encrypted (HTTPS). On your device, your session is
encrypted at rest (AES-256, key in the iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

## Your rights

- **Access/export:** contact us at the address below.
- **Deletion:** Settings → Delete account permanently deletes your account and
  all associated data.
- **Withdraw research consent:** Settings → research participation toggle.

## Children

RepSearch is not directed at children under 13 and the age rating reflects
user-generated content (12+/Teen).
